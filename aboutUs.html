About Us - Move Kenya

Move Kenya is a trusted relocation and logistics company built on a simple promise: to make every move—whether home, office, or business—smooth, efficient, and stress-free. We understand that moving isn’t just about transporting items; it’s about carrying people’s memories, livelihoods, and futures. From packing and storage to safe delivery and setup, we bring professionalism, care, and reliability to every stage of the journey. With a dedicated team of experienced movers, a modern fleet of vehicles, and transparent pricing, Move Kenya has become a preferred partner for individuals, families, and businesses across Kenya. We go beyond transport — we move dreams, connections, and opportunities, ensuring every client starts their next chapter with peace of mind.

Our Mission

To redefine relocation and logistics in Kenya by delivering seamless, affordable, and dependable moving services that put people first. We aim to simplify the moving experience through innovation, efficiency, and genuine care — ensuring every move is timely, safe, and hassle-free.

Our Vision

To be Kenya’s most trusted and customer-centered moving brand, recognized for reliability, excellence, and innovation in every service we offer. We envision a future where moving is not stressful but simple, smart, and secure, empowering individuals and businesses to relocate with confidence.

Our Values

Customer-Centricity: We prioritize our clients’ needs, ensuring personalized service and satisfaction at every step.

Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our dealings.

Reliability: We are committed to delivering on our promises, ensuring timely and safe moves.

Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve our services through technology and creative solutions.

Teamwork: We foster a collaborative environment where every team member is valued and empowered to contribute their best.

Sustainability: We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact through eco-friendly practices and responsible resource management.
